Ketupat

Description

Ketupat is a compact rice cake commonly enjoyed during festive meals. It is usually served with rendang, satay, or peanut sauce. This method uses ketupat casings and gives a firm but tender texture.

Ingredients

  • 10 woven ketupat casings
  • 4 cups uncooked white rice, rinsed and drained
  • Water for boiling
  • 1 tsp salt (optional)

Steps

  1. Fill each ketupat casing with rice to about half full. Do not overfill.
  2. Place the filled casings in a large pot and add enough water to fully cover them.
  3. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a steady simmer.
  4. Cook for 2 to 3 hours, topping up with hot water when needed so casings stay submerged.
  5. Remove ketupat and hang to drain and cool completely for several hours.
  6. Once cool, cut open the casing and slice the rice cake into cubes before serving.
