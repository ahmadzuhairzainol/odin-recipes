Lasagna

Description

This classic beef lasagna is layered with a rich tomato meat sauce, creamy ricotta filling, and melted mozzarella. It is a hearty dish that works well for family dinners or meal prep.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 500 g ground beef
  • 700 g tomato passata or pasta sauce
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 450 g ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 190C. Lightly oil a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. Cook lasagna noodles in salted boiling water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Cook onion until soft, then add garlic for 30 seconds.
  4. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Stir in passata, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta with egg and a pinch of salt.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the dish. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, meat sauce, and mozzarella. Repeat until used up.
  7. Top with remaining mozzarella and parmesan.
  8. Cover with foil and bake 25 minutes. Uncover and bake another 15 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  9. Rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
